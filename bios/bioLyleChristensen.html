Lyle Floyd "Thor" Christensen competes full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series, driving the No. 69 Chevrolet Camaro for Spire Motorsports. Christensen's sister Melinda is a fellow competitor in the NASCAR Cup Series. His nephews Sam and Max Turner, nieces MJ Nelson, Madeline Turner, and Melissa Nelson are also fellow competitors.


Season Summary

Clash

Christensen was 24th fastest in 1st practice, 6th fastest in 2nd practice, and 12th fastest in 3rd practice. He started in 3rd place in Heat 4 and finished 2nd to Gage Thiel to advance to the main event, while his teammate Kasen Kelander originally failed to advance, but later advanced through the Last Chance Qualifier. At the main event, Christensen started 8th. He finished 2nd place to Ty Nichols.
