Trackhouse Racing

Owner Justin Marks joined forces with rapper Pitbull to start the team. Their driver lineup is made up of a diverse set of drivers from different racing backgrounds. The team fields the No. 1 Chevrolet for Gabe Andrew who is a former TSA officer, the No. 99 Chevrolet for Steve Sorenson who is an experienced mechanic and has dirt racing family, and the No. 85 driven by Gage Thiel, who comes from an open-wheel and karting motorsport background.
