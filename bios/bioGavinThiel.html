Gavin Thiel competes full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series, driving the No. 23 Ford Mustang Dark Horse for privately-owned race team GOAT Racing.


Season Summary

Clash

Thiel was 17th fastest in 1st practice, 18th fastest in 2nd practice, and 1st fastest in 3rd practice. He started on pole in Heat 1, in which he led all 25 laps and won the race to advance to the main event. At the main event, Thiel started on pole and led the first 45 laps until Morgan Cloud took the lead. Thiel finished the race in 4th.

Daytona 500

Thiel was 33rd fastest in qualifying. Thiel started 17th in Duel 1.
