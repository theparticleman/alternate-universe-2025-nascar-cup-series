Trevan Haws competes full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series, driving the No. 17 Ford Mustang Dark Horse for RFK Racing. His twin brother Tosh competes in the Cup Series as well.


Season Summary

Clash

Haws was the fastest in 1st practice, 8th fastest in 2nd practice, and 23rd fastest in 3rd practice. He started in 6th place in Heat 3. Haws finished last in his heat race and did not advance to the main event. At the Last Chance Qualifier, Haws started 15th. On lap 59, race leader Melissa Nelson crashed into the wall, handing the lead over to Trevan who held it for one lap until getting passed by Jon Turner. Haws finished 9th, failing to advance to the main event.
