Melinda June Nelson (born September 8, 2009) is an American professional stock car racing driver. She competes full-time in the NASCAR Cup Series, driving the No. 5 Chevrolet Camaro for Hendrick Motorsports.


Personal life

Nelson's sister Melissa is a fellow competitor in the NASCAR Cup Series. Her cousins Sam, Madeline, and Max Turner, and their uncle, Lyle Christensen, are also fellow competitors.
